Features
[+]

Waveform Visualization

See your audio and video with stunning waveform displays. Choose between filled and line styles.

[+]

Speech to Text

Transcribe audio using Apple's on-device AI. Fast, private, no internet required.

[+]

Precise Editing

Trim your clips with precision using visual trim handles or enter exact timestamps.

[+]

Multiple Export Formats

Export audio to WAV, FLAC, or M4A. Export video to MP4 with customizable quality.

[+]

Customizable Themes

Personalize your workspace with custom colors and themes that match your style.

[+]

Native macOS App

Built with SwiftUI for a fast, responsive experience that feels right at home on your Mac.
